## Executive Summary

The dashboard addresses a $50-150M annual market (1,400 FQHCs x $35K-$100K average contract). Tiered pricing model aligns with FQHC size (small, mid, large) and budget constraints. Conservative estimates project $5M ARR Year 3 with 50 customers.

**Pricing Strategy:** Value-based (priced on time savings + denial reduction), not cost-plus
**GTM Motion:** Direct sales to FQHCs via NACHC partnerships, pilot-first approach
**Customer Acquisition:** $15K CAC (conferences, demos, pilot FQHCs as references)

## Pricing Model

### Tier 1: Small FQHCs (1-3 sites)
**Target Customer:**
- 1-3 clinic sites
- 2-5 BH providers
- Director + small admin team
- Budget: Limited (~$20K discretionary spend)

**Pricing:**
- **$5,000/year** (monthly: $500)
- Includes:
  - Morning Brief module
  - Capacity Planning (up to 5 providers)
  - Risk Compliance (credentialing alerts, authorization tracking)
  - Basic audit reports
  - 1 EHR integration (Epic, NextGen, or eCW)
  - Email support

**Value Proposition:**
- Save 10 hours/week on manual data aggregation (director time = $60/hr x 10 hrs x 52 weeks = $31K/year)
- Reduce denial rate by 5% (avg 100 visits/week x $200/visit x 5% x 52 weeks = $52K/year)
- **Total value: ~$83K/year for $5K investment (16x ROI)**

**Expected Adoption:** 200 FQHCs (40% of small FQHCs)
**ARR Contribution:** $1M

### Tier 2: Mid-Sized FQHCs (4-10 sites)
**Target Customer:**
- 4-10 clinic sites
- 8-15 BH providers
- Director + clinical supervisors + admin staff
- Budget: Moderate (~$50K discretionary spend)

**Pricing:**
- **$25,000/year** (monthly: $2,500)
- Includes:
  - All Tier 1 features
  - RCM Performance module (denials, claims aging, appeal workflows)
  - Team Management module (staff scheduling, productivity tracking)
  - Analytics & Reporting
  - Up to 15 providers
  - 2 EHR integrations + 1 billing system integration
  - Priority support (24-hour response time)
  - Quarterly business reviews

**Value Proposition:**
- Save 15 hours/week on manual aggregation + denial management (director time = $60/hr x 15 hrs x 52 weeks = $47K/year)
- Reduce denial rate by 10% (avg 400 visits/week x $200/visit x 10% x 52 weeks = $416K/year)
- Reduce staff turnover by 10% (avg 2 departures/year x $75K replacement cost x 10% = $15K/year)
- **Total value: ~$478K/year for $25K investment (19x ROI)**

**Expected Adoption:** 70 FQHCs (20% of mid-sized FQHCs)
**ARR Contribution:** $1.75M

### Tier 3: Large FQHCs (11+ sites)
**Target Customer:**
- 11+ clinic sites
- 15-25+ BH providers
- Director + associate directors + program managers
- Budget: Large (~$150K+ discretionary spend)

**Pricing:**
- **$75,000/year** (monthly: $7,500)
- Includes:
  - All Tier 2 features
  - Multi-location dashboard (rollup views across sites)
  - Advanced analytics (predictive capacity planning, denial forecasting)
  - Custom integrations (beyond standard EHR/billing)
  - Unlimited providers
  - Dedicated account manager
  - White-glove onboarding (on-site training)
  - Dedicated Slack channel (real-time support)

**Value Proposition:**
- Save 20 hours/week on manual aggregation + reporting (director + associate directors = $60/hr x 20 hrs x 52 weeks = $62K/year)
- Reduce denial rate by 15% (avg 1,000 visits/week x $200/visit x 15% x 52 weeks = $1.56M/year)
- Reduce staff turnover by 15% (avg 5 departures/year x $75K replacement cost x 15% = $56K/year)
- **Total value: ~$1.68M/year for $75K investment (22x ROI)**

**Expected Adoption:** 15 FQHCs (12% of large FQHCs)
**ARR Contribution:** $1.125M

### Enterprise (Custom Pricing)
**Target Customer:**
- Multi-state FQHC networks (e.g., Community Health Center, Inc. - 12 states, 300+ sites)
- >50 BH providers
- Complex integration needs (legacy systems, custom EHRs)

**Pricing:**
- **$150K-$500K/year** (custom quote)
- Includes:
  - All Tier 3 features
  - Custom development (bespoke integrations, features)
  - Dedicated engineering support
  - SLA guarantees (99.9% uptime)
  - Annual health checks

**Expected Adoption:** 5 networks (Year 3+)
**ARR Contribution:** $1M (conservative)

## Customer Acquisition Strategy

### Phase 1: Pilot FQHCs (Year 1 Q1-Q2)
**Goal:** Validate product-market fit, build case studies

**Approach:**
- Recruit 5 pilot FQHCs (varying sizes)
- Free during 90-day pilot
- Intensive feedback loops (weekly check-ins)
- Measure outcomes:
  - Time savings (hrs/week)
  - Denial rate reduction (%)
  - Director satisfaction (NPS score)

**Selection Criteria:**
- Must use Epic, NextGen, or eCW (priority EHR integrations)
- Must have >5 BH providers (enough complexity to test features)
- Must have engaged director (willing to provide feedback)
- Geographic diversity (rural, urban, different states)

**Investment:** $100K (eng time for pilots, travel for onboarding)

### Phase 2: NACHC Partnership (Year 1 Q3-Q4)
**Goal:** Gain credibility, access FQHC network

**Approach:**
- Sponsor NACHC Community Health Institute (CHI) conference (~$25K)
- Present case studies from pilot FQHCs
- Demo booth (live dashboard walkthrough)
- Offer conference discount ($4K small, $20K mid, $60K large for first year)

**Expected Results:**
- 200 demos at conference
- 50 qualified leads (director interested, has budget authority)
- 10 paid customers signed (Year 1 Q4 go-live)

**Investment:** $50K (sponsorship, booth, travel)

### Phase 3: State Primary Care Associations (Year 2+)
**Goal:** Regional expansion, state-level endorsements

**Approach:**
- Partner with state associations (e.g., California Primary Care Association, Texas Association of Community Health Centers)
- Webinars for members ("How to Reduce BH Denial Rates by 10%")
- Pilot programs (state association subsidizes 50% for members)
- Case studies featuring in-state FQHCs

**Expected Results:**
- 100 webinar attendees per state (10 target states = 1,000 total)
- 50 qualified leads per state
- 20 paid customers signed per state (Year 2 = 200 total)

**Investment:** $200K (partnerships, webinars, subsidies)

### Phase 4: Direct Sales (Year 2+)
**Goal:** Scale beyond conferences, build sales team

**Approach:**
- Hire 2 SDRs (Sales Development Reps) - outbound prospecting
- Hire 2 AEs (Account Executives) - demo + close
- Cold outreach (email, LinkedIn) targeting BH directors
- Free demo offer ("See how we reduce denial rates by 10%")

**Sales Cycle:**
- Week 1: SDR reaches out, qualifies lead
- Week 2: AE demo (30 minutes, live dashboard)
- Week 3: AE follow-up (answer questions, custom pricing quote)
- Week 4: Contract signed (or lost)
- **Average sales cycle: 4 weeks (short for healthcare B2B)**

**Quota:**
- SDR: 50 qualified leads/month
- AE: 5 closed deals/month (10% close rate)

**Investment:** $500K/year (4 salespeople x $125K fully loaded)

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Small FQHC:**
- Annual revenue: $5K
- Average lifetime: 5 years (80% retention = 5-year avg lifespan)
- **LTV: $25K**
- **LTV:CAC ratio: 1.7x** (below target 3x, but acceptable for early stage)

**Mid-Sized FQHC:**
- Annual revenue: $25K
- Average lifetime: 7 years (85% retention = 7-year avg lifespan)
- **LTV: $175K**
- **LTV:CAC ratio: 11.7x** (excellent)

**Large FQHC:**
- Annual revenue: $75K
- Average lifetime: 10 years (90% retention = 10-year avg lifespan)
- **LTV: $750K**
- **LTV:CAC ratio: 50x** (exceptional)

**Blended (assuming 40% small, 40% mid, 20% large):**
- **LTV: $180K**
- **LTV:CAC ratio: 12x** (very strong)

**Strategic Implication:** Focus sales effort on mid/large FQHCs (higher LTV), use low-touch/PLG for small FQHCs.

## Competitive Pricing Analysis

### Epic EHR (with BH module)
**Pricing:** $500K-$2M implementation + $50K-$200K/year maintenance
**Strengths:** Comprehensive EHR, strong FQHC billing support
**Weaknesses:** Poor BH workflows, expensive, long implementation (12-18 months)
**Our Advantage:** 10x cheaper ($25K vs $250K/year mid-sized), faster deployment (90 days)

### Valant (BH-specific EHR)
**Pricing:** $200/provider/month = $24K/year for 10 providers
**Strengths:** Great BH workflows, measurement-based care
**Weaknesses:** Weak FQHC billing support, no capacity planning, no compliance dashboards
**Our Advantage:** Same price, but includes capacity planning + compliance + RCM

### athenahealth (cloud-based EHR + RCM)
**Pricing:** 4-8% of collections (avg FQHC BH = $2M revenue = $80K-$160K/year)
**Strengths:** Strong RCM, cloud-based, good FQHC support
**Weaknesses:** Percentage pricing (grows with revenue), weak BH workflows
**Our Advantage:** Fixed pricing ($25K/year vs $120K/year avg), BH-first design

### Kareo (practice management + billing)
**Pricing:** $160/provider/month = $19.2K/year for 10 providers
**Strengths:** Affordable, good billing features
**Weaknesses:** No clinical EHR, weak compliance features, no capacity planning
**Our Advantage:** Similar price, but includes EHR integration + compliance + capacity planning

**Positioning:** "The only dashboard that makes FQHC behavioral health audit-ready without rip-and-replace integration."
