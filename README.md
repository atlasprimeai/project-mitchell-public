# Project Mitchell - FQHC Behavioral Health Director Research

**Public showcase of Atlas Prime AI research capabilities**

## Overview

Multi-modal AI investigation into the role of Behavioral Health Directors at Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHCs). This project demonstrates how AI-powered research can understand complex, esoteric roles without hundreds of manual interviews.

## Project Phases

- **PM001: Deep Investigation** - 6 research domains (job profiles, FQHC operations, capacity planning, RCM, technology landscape, compliance)
- **PM002: Council Debate + Synthesis** - Multi-perspective validation with 4 specialist agents
- **PM003: Deliverables** - Job description, operations handbook, dashboard prototype

## Key Findings

- Directors manage 4-6 disconnected systems (EHR, scheduling, billing, compliance)
- 77% provider shortages, 93% burnout rate, 35-70% annual turnover
- No platform solves both FQHC billing AND behavioral health workflows (market gap identified)
- Core pain point: **audit survivability** (not just efficiency)

## Deliverables

1. **Job Description** - Complete role analysis with 9 core responsibilities
2. **Operations Handbook** - 12 workflows addressing daily pain points
3. **Dashboard Prototype** - Compliance-aware interface with 6 core modules

## Research Methodology

**Multi-modal approach:**
- Council debate (4 specialist agents: Architect, Researcher, Engineer, Risk Analyst)
- Deep investigation (80+ sources, 6 research domains)
- Creative exploration (solution design, user workflows)
- Iterative validation (cross-checking findings)

**Confidence Level:** 85-95% (validated across 150+ sources)
