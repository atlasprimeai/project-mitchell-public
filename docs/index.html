🎯 Mission & Context

Problem: AI models lack context on esoteric roles like behavioral health directors at rural FQHCs. Traditional approach would require hundreds of interview hours.

Approach: Multi-modal AI research combining council debate, deep investigation, creative exploration, and iterative validation to understand the role without extensive human interviews.

Goal: Build job description, day-to-day handbook, and dashboard prototype that makes their work easier.

📋 Research Objectives

  • Job Description Analysis — What this role actually entails in day-to-day operations
  • Success Handbook — How behavioral health directors think about their work and measure success
  • Dashboard Prototype — Modern, accessible UX that streamlines their critical workflows
